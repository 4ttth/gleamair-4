About Gleamair Enterprises

Integrated solutions for smarter spaces — a trusted name in HVAC and building solutions, built on over eight years of technical expertise.

Our Story

Providing Exceptional Solutions Since Day One

Gleamair Enterprises is a leading provider of lighting, ventilation, and air conditioning solutions for both residential and commercial clients throughout the Philippines. With the owner bringing over eight years of deep industry experience, we have built a reputation for delivering high-quality, dependable results.

We specialize in complete HVAC installations, ongoing maintenance programs, and emergency service responses — all tailored to meet the unique operational needs of each client. Our approach is rooted in the belief that comfort and efficiency should never be compromised.

Headquartered in Olongapo City, We serve clients across the Philippines and beyond, offering customized solutions that range from standard air conditioning units to advanced wind-driven roof ventilation systems and daylighting technology.

Our partnership with leading manufacturers ensures that our clients have access to products backed by proven engineering and long-term warranties.
