Featured Product

Green-Vent Wind — Wind Driven Roof Ventilators

The Green-Vent Wind is a premium wind-driven roof ventilation system developed using Australian engineering and manufactured locally in the Philippines by SG Eco Industries Inc.

Designed to operate without electricity, the Green-Vent Wind uses the natural energy of wind to continuously expel hot, stale air from within a building — replacing it with cooler, fresher air from outside. Even minimal wind speeds are sufficient to activate the system.

With a 15-year manufacturer's warranty and a proven track record in demanding Philippine conditions — including heavy monsoon rainfall — the Green-Vent Wind is one of the most reliable roof ventilation solutions available in the market.

Australian Technology Made in Philippines 15-Year Warranty

Product Advantages

  • Vertical Vane Technology for superior performance at all wind speeds
  • Up to 3 times the exhaust capacity of conventional round ventilators
  • Lightweight, high-strength aluminium construction — rust-free
  • Available in three sizes for cost-effective specification
  • Operates without electricity — zero ongoing energy cost
  • Year-round ventilation regardless of season
  • High-precision, quality-grade bearing assemblies
  • Larger exhaust openings for enhanced ventilation performance
  • Proven rain resistance — no leakage during heavy rainfall
  • 15-year manufacturer's warranty (see terms for details)
